Taco Bowl

Description

This is a go to recipe if you love tacos, but don't love the mess they create!

    Ingredients

    Taco Seasoning

  • 1 1/2 lbs. lean ground beef
  • 1 Tbsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper

    • Toppings

  • 1 15 oz. can black beans drained and rinsed
  • 1 15 oz. can whole corn drained
  • pico de gallo garnish
  • avocado garnish
  • cheddar cheese grated
  • cilantro garnish
  • brown rice approx. 1 1/2 cups per serving

    Steps

  1. Brown ground beef in a large skillet.
  2. Mix together seasonings and sprinkle over meat. Stir to incorporate. Simmer for 3-5 minutes
  3. Add cooked rice to individual serving bowl.
  4. Top with ground beef mixture. Add toppings â€“ pico, avocado, cheese, corn, black beans, and cilantro.
